Swords
Longsword

a european sword characterized as having a cruciform hilt with a grip for primarily two-handed use

Shortsword

Similar to the Longsword but not as long and generally used as a secondary weapon

Zweihander

The Zweihander is a large German two-handed sword primarily in use during the 16th century

Katana

The katana is a Japanese sword characterized by a curved, single-edged blade with a circular or squared guard and long grip to accommodate two hands
